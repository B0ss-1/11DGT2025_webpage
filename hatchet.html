The Hatchet is the character that I play out of the four characters. This Inox throws hatchets at enemys, hence the name hatchet. He has an interesting mechanic where he can use his favourite thowing axe for three more damage to the target of any ranged attack, but losing the abiltity to do so until he regains the axe from the battlefield. This creates a tension between collecting the favourite axe and other actions, such as moving and attacking. If you play this class, you will most likely have a lot more money tokens that everyone else because of this.
